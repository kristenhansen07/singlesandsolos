IMPORTANT: Passports are recommended for this trip. Please discuss specific document requirements when you book your cruise. Schedule/itinerary is subject to change.

Costs:
  • $100/person to cover all event costs. This non-refundable registration cost is paid to the ministry and is required to secure your Registration ID# for our group.

  • Cost for the cruise cabin and related fees. All cruise costs/reservations are handled directly with Royal Caribbean. Room price depends upon cabin selection. Feel free to “bunk” with friends also going on the cruise to save money. Current prices are as low as $320.72 per person (for 4 people to a room). When you register, you can inquire about available rooms for a lower fee or upgrades to suites. A $200 deposit will be required to reserve your room and the balance must be paid by February 5, 2017.

  • On-board food and beverages are included in cruise cost. All additional activities, excursions, spa treatments, etc. are additional costs. Also, all on-shore food and beverages are additional costs with the exception of CocoCay, which is owned by Royal Caribbean.

  • Gratuities are required on board the ship and are charged automatically. Current estimates are $13.50-$16.50 per person per day. You can pre-pay these when you book your cruise or choose to be billed daily on your SeaPass while on board.

  • Depending on how you will travel to and from the cruise port, you may also have costs to park at the terminal.
